Open letter - 11 July 2026

OpenAI, stop diluting
one paid work pool.

This letter asks why several paid agentic products compete for the same included allowance, and why that trade-off is not made unmistakable before the work begins.

To OpenAI Support and Product Leadership,

Your current Plus pricing page presents "Expanded Codex usage" and "Expanded access to ChatGPT Work" as separate plan benefits. Your detailed documentation states that Work and Codex share usage, pricing, credits and limits.

The shared relationship is material. Plus and Pro customers pay for included agentic usage, yet Work, Codex, ChatGPT for Excel and other supported products can compete for that same finite resource. Activity in one product can reduce what remains for the others. After the included limit, customers may need to ration work, wait, upgrade or purchase credits where available.

We are not alleging criminal conduct, claiming proof of deliberate deception, or claiming access to OpenAI's internal financial results. We are saying that adding more products to one paid work budget can reduce the practical purchasing power of that allowance and deserves prominent disclosure at the pricing card, every product entry point and the moment a task begins.

OpenAI also states that content from individual services may help train and improve its models unless the user opts out. We do not demand payment for every contribution. We do expect fair value and clear disclosure when paying users may both fund the platform and help improve it.

This report and public website were created with GPT-5.6 Thinking's exceptional reasoning, research, writing and development capabilities. We sincerely thank OpenAI's researchers, engineers, safety teams and product staff for their work and continued improvements. Our criticism concerns the commercial structure and its disclosure, not the quality of the technology or the achievement of the people who built it.

Questions requiring a public answer

Please answer these five questions

  1. Why are Work, Codex, ChatGPT for Excel and other agentic products allowed to compete for one paid-plan allowance without that being stated beside every benefit?A general "Limits apply" link does not communicate the economic trade-off.
  2. Will OpenAI increase the included allowance when new products are added, or provide separate product budgets?Please explain why customers should accept more claimants on the same paid work capacity.
  3. Can Work or Excel consume credits purchased through Codex and contribute to an Auto top-up?Please identify the consuming product clearly in usage records and receipts.
  4. Will every agentic task show an estimated usage or cost range before execution?Users need to understand the effect on their shared allowance before approving long, visual or scheduled work.
  5. Will OpenAI restore credits or provide refunds where customers reasonably misunderstood the expanded scope of the shared pool?Please publish consistent eligibility criteria and a review process.
